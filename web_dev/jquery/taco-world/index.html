A Bit of Taco World History

We got started in 1949 as a small mom-and-pop shop in Los Angeles, California. And now we have 14 locations across the United States. The cool thing is that each shop still has its own mom-and-pop feel to it, so we haven't forgetten our roots. We hope you enjoy our tacos as much as we do!
